Project 3: Parametric Flower

Design Process

  • This process began with a sketch in Illustrator and defining what my parameters/variables will be and how they will be changed. I incorporated the p5.Polar into my web editor files to simplify the radial drawing process.
  • Sketches made in Illustrator

    • Reflections

    The process of creating the shapes shapes were made easier by adding the p5.polar library into this sketch. It helped me plot points on a circle without needing to use trigonometry. Defining variable and assigning mouse locations was pretty simple as well. The most difficult part was the randomization of the color that was changed through mouse clicks.
