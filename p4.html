Project 4: Experimental Clock

Design Process + Reflections

This was my interpretation of a different way of tracking passed time. It was inspired by the growth pattern of tree rings when looking at the cross-section of a tree trunk. The rings grow from its center, and its color is dependent on the change in seasons/weather. Counting tree rings is how people are able to estimate the age of a tree. This process began with a sketch in Illustrator and defining what my variables will be and how they will be changed. I was able to use loops and conditionals in this sketch as well.
